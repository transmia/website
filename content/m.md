# DIY Injectable Estradiol Enanthate (50 mg/ml) Recipe  
**MtF HRT - One 10 ml Vial**  
**Yield:** 10 ml at 50 mg/ml (500 mg total EEn)

## Ingredients (for one 10 ml vial)

- **Estradiol Enanthate powder**: 0.501 - 0.503 g (target 0.5 g)  
- **Benzyl Alcohol** (optional preservative): 0.2 ml  
- **MCT oil** (C8/C10 mix, liquid, unflavored, no additives):  
  - 9.35 ml if using benzyl alcohol  
  - 9.55 ml if skipping benzyl alcohol (for allergy)

### Smaller Batch Alternative (8 ml vial - recommended if volume doesn’t fit)
- Estradiol Enanthate: 0.4 g  
- Benzyl Alcohol (optional): 0.16 ml  
- MCT oil: 7.5 ml (with BA) or 7.65 ml (without BA)

## Tools & Equipment

- Digital pocket scale (0.001 g / 1 mg precision, max 10-50 g)  
- 10 ml clear glass vial with 20 mm grey or red butyl rubber stopper and aluminium cap  
- 10 ml or 5 ml all-plastic syringe (no rubber on plunger) for measuring oil  
- Insulin syringes (U-100, ½" / 12-13 mm fixed needle) for injections  
- Funnel made from kitchen foil (≈ 5 cm square) or thicker foil  
- Small knife or tweezers for handling powder  
- Plastic pen or wooden pencil (for tapping)  
- Blank sheet of paper (landscape orientation)  
- Book (for crimping)  
- Spoon handle (for crimping the cap)  
- Pot (or pressure cooker) for sterilization  
- Cotton thread (if sterilizing multiple vials)  
- Cloth + vinegar (to clean limescale)  
- Paper + transparent sticky tape (for labeling)  
- Kitchen foil (to protect from light)

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Prepare workspace**  
   Sit at a table. Place the scale in the center of a blank sheet of paper (landscape). Turn on the scale and wait for it to read 0.000.

2. **Make the funnel**  
   Cut or fold a small square of kitchen foil (shiny side inside if possible). Shape it into a funnel.

3. **Weigh the powder**  
   Place the funnel on the scale over the center circle.  
   Using the tip of a small knife, carefully add Estradiol Enanthate powder until the scale shows **0.501 - 0.503 g**.  
   Remove any excess with the knife tip. Don’t worry about tiny amounts that fall onto the paper.

4. **Transfer powder to vial**  
   Hold the empty vial in your left hand.  
   Press the pointy end of the funnel lightly into the vial opening with your left index finger.  
   Rotate the vial upright while keeping the funnel in place.  
   Tap the side of the vial gently multiple times with a pen or pencil until all powder falls into the vial.

5. **Add preservative (optional)**  
   Draw **0.2 ml** benzyl alcohol into a small syringe and add it to the vial over the powder. The powder will sag slightly.

6. **Add MCT oil**  
   Using the larger syringe, measure and slowly add the correct amount of MCT oil:  
   - **9.35 ml** (with benzyl alcohol) or  
   - **9.55 ml** (without benzyl alcohol).  
   Leave a small air gap at the top.

7. **Seal the vial**  
   Insert the rubber stopper firmly.  
   Place the aluminium cap on top.  
   Turn the vial upside down (cap on a book).  
   While pressing down, use the spoon handle to bend the rim of the cap inward in **8-10 places** so it holds the stopper securely. Do not press too hard (risk of breaking glass).

8. **Shake thoroughly**  
   Hold the vial and shake vigorously for a **full 1 minute**.

9. **Dissolve the powder**  
   Let the vial sit at room temperature for up to several days.  
   Shake the first vial a few times each day.  
   Warmer room temperature speeds up dissolution.  
   The solution should become completely clear when fully dissolved.  
   (If white sediment appears later, warm the vial and shake — it will redissolve.)

10. **Sterilize**  
    Place the sealed vial upright in a pot with tap water (water up to half the vial height).  
    If doing multiple vials, tie them with cotton thread.  
    Bring the water to a boil and keep boiling for **30 minutes**.  
    Turn off heat and let it cool naturally.  
    **Alternative:** Use a pressure cooker (same 30 min).  
    After sterilization, wash any limescale off the vial with a cloth wet with vinegar. Let it dry.

11. **Label the vial**  
    Write “Estradiol Enanthate 50 mg/ml” on paper and tape it securely to the vial.  
    Wrap the vial in foil to protect from light.

## Storage

- Store the finished vial upright at **room temperature** (not in the fridge) in a drawer, box, or cupboard.  
- Keep unused powder and vials wrapped in foil away from light.  
- Before each use, check the bottom: if white, warm the vial and shake until clear again.

## Usage & Dosage

- **Weekly:** 0.22 ml (22 units on a U-100 insulin syringe)  
- **Every 10 days:** 0.31 ml (e.g. on the 1st, 11th, 21st of each month)  
- Inject **subcutaneously** into the upper outer quarter of the buttock fat while lying on your side.  
- Use a **new insulin syringe** each time.  
- Start with this dose from the beginning of HRT and continue indefinitely (including post-op).

**Important Notes:**
- This is a terminal heat-sterilized product.  
- Always use sterile injection technique.  
- Research thoroughly and understand the risks before proceeding.
