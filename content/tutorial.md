## STUFF YOULL NEED

- Digital pockett scale (0.001 g/mg prec., max 10-50g because higher have less sensitivity and we need more sensitivity)
- 10ml clear glass vial with 20mm grey or red butyl rubber stopper and aluminium cpa
- 10ml or 5ml all plastic syringe (NO be rubber on plunger) for measuring the oil
- Insulin syringes (U-100, 1/2" / 12-13mm fixed needle) for injections
- Funnle made from kitchen foil (around 5cm2) or thicker foil
- Small knife or tweezers for handling powder
- Plastic pen or wooden pencil (for tapping)
- Blank sheet of paper (landscape orientation later)
- Book (for crimping)
- Spoon handle (for crimping the cap)
- Pot (or pressure cooker) for sterilization
- Cotton thread (if sterilizing multiple vials)
- Cloth + vinegar (to clean limescale)
- Paper + transparent sticky tape (for labeling, optional)
- Kitchen foil (to protect from light)
